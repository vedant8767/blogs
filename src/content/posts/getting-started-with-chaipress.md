Welcome to Chaipress! This guide will walk you through setting up your first site from scratch.

## Why Chaipress?

Chaipress is a **page-led publishing system** built on Astro and CloudCannon. It gives you the speed of a static site with the editing power of a CMS — no coding required for day-to-day content changes.

### Key Benefits

* **Visual Editing** — See your changes in real-time with CloudCannon's visual editor
* **Page Templates** — Start with pre-built templates for landing pages, docs, courses, and more
* **Blazing Fast** — Static output means sub-second page loads
* **SEO Optimized** — Built-in meta tags, Open Graph, and structured data

## Quick Start

### 1\. Connect Your Repository

Link your GitHub, GitLab, or Bitbucket repository to CloudCannon. The platform auto-detects the Astro configuration and sets up the build pipeline.

### 2\. Choose a Template

When creating a new page, you'll see six ready-made templates:

\| Template \| Best For \| \|----------\|----------\| \| SaaS Landing \| Product marketing pages \| \| Course Sales \| Online course promotion \| \| Docs Homepage \| Documentation portals \| \| Webinar Registration \| Event sign-ups \| \| About / Team \| Company information \| \| Pricing \| Plan comparison pages \|

### 3\. Customize Your Content

Every section on a page is a **content block** that you can:

* **Edit** — Click any text to change it directly
* **Reorder** — Drag sections up or down
* **Add** — Insert new sections from the component library
* **Remove** — Delete sections you don't need

### 4\. Publish

Hit the **Save** button and CloudCannon will build and deploy your site automatically. Changes go live in under 60 seconds.
